About Us

Welcome to Giles waley Guest House Booni, a peaceful hideaway in the breathtaking valleys of Booni, Chitral — Northern Pakistan, surrounded by majestic peaks and untouched natural beauty. Our guesthouse is a place where comfort, culture, and adventure come together to create an unforgettable experience.

offer five spacious and beautifully maintained rooms, each with its own neat and clean private washroom, ensuring complete comfort and privacy. The guesthouse also features a large meeting hall, perfect for gatherings or small events, and a cozy dining hall where guests can enjoy delicious local and traditional Chitrali food, freshly prepared with love and local ingredients.

For those who love to cook, we provide a fully equipped guest kitchen — so you can prepare your own meals if you wish. With free Wi-Fi, 24-hour hot water, and fresh seasonal fruits, we make sure your stay feels like home, only more peaceful.

Step outside, and nature welcomes you. Our guesthouse overlooks a beautiful garden and offers a serene riverside view where guests can relax, enjoy the mountain breeze, or spend the night under the stars at our dedicated camping site.

Adventure seekers will find their thrill here — paragliding and horse riding are our signature experiences, giving you a chance to see Chitral’s landscapes from new and breathtaking perspectives.

Whether you’re here to rest, explore, or reconnect with nature, Giles waley promises comfort, cleanliness, and a true taste of Northern Pakistan’s hospitality.
